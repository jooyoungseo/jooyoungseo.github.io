Accessible Data Science for the Blind Using R

Apr 3, 2018 9 min read Data Science

Three Available Packages Specialized for Visually Impaired R Users

There are three packages for the blind R users (Godfrey, Warren, Murrell, Bilton, & Sorge, 2020; Seo, 2020; Siegert & Williams, 2017).

Accessible Math Test

The following shows an accessible Math created by R along with MathJax:

\[\frac {2} {3} \]

\(3+2=5\)

\[ 3*2=6 \]

\[5^2=25\]

Data Textualization
